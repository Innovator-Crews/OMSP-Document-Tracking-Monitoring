Platform Features

Everything you need to manage legislative records

Purpose-built tools for the Sangguniang Panlalawigan to streamline document tracking, compliance monitoring, and budget oversight.

FA Record Management

Create, track, and manage Financial Assistance records with full audit trails, category tagging, and compliance status tracking.

PA Record Tracking

Monitor Personal Assistance records for each board member with detailed entries, searchable archives, and progress indicators.

Budget Monitoring

Real-time budget allocation, utilization tracking, and per-member financial reports with visual breakdowns and trend analysis.

Global Search

Powerful full-text search across all records, categories, and board members with advanced filters and instant results.

Analytics & Reports

Generate comprehensive reports with charts and export options. Track compliance rates, submission trends, and category distribution.

Role-Based Access

Secure authentication with role-specific permissions for System Admins, Board Members, and Secretary Staff.
